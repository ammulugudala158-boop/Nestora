# Prompt for AI App Builder

Build a modern full-stack web application called Nestora** that helps small businesses improve their digital presence and customer experience.**

The application must have **Role-Based Authentication** with two completely separate dashboards:

## 1. Business Owner Portal (Admin Dashboard)

Only business owners can access this dashboard after logging in.

Features:

* Business Profile Management
* Edit company information, logo, address, contact details, working hours
* Website customization
* Product & Service Management
* Add/Edit/Delete products and services
* Upload product images
* Manage pricing and stock
* Create categories
* Offer & Discount Management
* Create festival offers
* Flash sales
* Coupons
* Loyalty rewards
* Schedule offers automatically
* Order Management
* View all customer orders
* Update order status (Pending, Processing, Shipped, Delivered)
* Analytics Dashboard
* Total Sales
* Monthly Revenue
* Profit
* Expenses
* Customer Growth
* Best Selling Products
* Website Visitors
* Marketing Dashboard
* Social media post scheduler
* Email campaign creator
* SMS and WhatsApp campaign management
* Customer Management
* View customer list
* Purchase history
* Loyalty points
* Customer feedback
* Support Ticket Management
* View customer complaints
* Reply to customer queries
* Business Reports
* Download PDF/Excel reports

**Only the Business Owner can view:**

* Revenue
* Profit
* Expenses
* Analytics
* Employee data
* Customer database
* Marketing reports
* Sales reports
* Business settings

Customers must NEVER be able to access this information.

---

## 2. Customer Portal

Customers should have a clean, attractive interface.

Features:

* Sign Up / Login
* Browse Products & Services
* Search and Filter
* View Product Details
* Add to Cart
* Place Orders
* Track Orders in Real Time
* View Current Offers and Discounts
* Festival Deals
* Coupon Codes
* Loyalty Points
* Reward History
* Personalized Recommendations
* Save Favorite Products
* Chat with Business
* Raise Support Tickets
* Rate Products
* Write Reviews
* Receive Notifications
* View Business Contact Details
* Book Appointments (if service business)

Customers must NOT see:

* Profit
* Revenue
* Expenses
* Sales Analytics
* Customer List
* Marketing Reports
* Employee Information
* Business Settings
* Admin Dashboard

---

## Authentication

Implement secure Role-Based Access Control (RBAC).

Roles:

* Owner (Admin)
* Customer

After login:

* Owners are redirected to the Admin Dashboard.
* Customers are redirected to the Customer Dashboard.

Unauthorized users should never be able to access admin pages, even by typing URLs manually.

---

## Backend

Use:

* Node.js
* Express.js
* MongoDB
* JWT Authentication
* bcrypt Password Encryption

Create APIs for:

* Authentication
* Products
* Orders
* Offers
* Loyalty
* Reviews
* Analytics
* Customer Support
* Notifications

Protect every admin API using JWT and role verification.
